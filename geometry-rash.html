About Geometry Rash

Geometry Rash is an addictive rhythm-based platformer where you control a geometric character through a series of increasingly challenging levels. The game combines precise platforming with music synchronization, creating a gameplay experience where obstacles, jumps, and hazards all flow with the energetic soundtrack.

With its vibrant visuals, pulsing electronic music, and simple yet challenging mechanics, Geometry Rash offers an engaging experience that requires focus, rhythm, and reflexes. Each level presents new obstacles and patterns to master, with difficulty progressively increasing as you advance through the game. Play directly in your browser with no downloads required.

Game Features

  • Rhythm-based platforming synchronized with energetic music
  • Multiple game modes including normal, practice, and challenge
  • Variety of geometric characters to unlock and play
  • Increasingly difficult levels with unique obstacle patterns
  • Colorful, vibrant visual effects that pulse with the music
  • Achievement system to track your progress
  • Level editor to create and share your own challenges
  • Play instantly in your browser - no downloads needed

How to Play

  • Press spacebar, up arrow, or mouse click to jump
  • Hold to jump higher or fly (in some game modes)
  • Time your jumps with the music rhythm for best results
  • Avoid obstacles like spikes, saws, and moving hazards
  • Hit jump pads to launch higher or portals to change gravity
  • Collect coins to unlock new characters and features
  • Complete levels to progress to more challenging stages
  • Use practice mode to master difficult sections of levels
