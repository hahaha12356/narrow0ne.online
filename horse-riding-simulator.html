Play Horse Riding Simulator Now

Experience the joy of horseback riding in this immersive equestrian simulator. Bond with your virtual horse, explore beautiful landscapes, complete riding challenges, and improve your skills - all in your browser!

About Horse Riding Simulator

Horse Riding Simulator offers a captivating equestrian experience that combines realistic horse riding mechanics with beautiful environments to explore. Choose from different horses, each with unique characteristics, and build a bond with your virtual companion as you navigate through various terrains and challenges.

The game features multiple modes including free roaming exploration, structured riding lessons, challenging courses, and competitive events. As you progress, you'll unlock new horses, equipment, and riding locations. With its attention to realistic riding mechanics and the relationship between rider and horse, this simulator provides both entertainment and an introduction to the world of equestrian sports.

Game Features

  • Multiple horse breeds with unique attributes
  • Realistic riding mechanics and horse behavior
  • Beautiful landscapes and varied environments to explore
  • Training modes to improve your riding skills
  • Competitive events including dressage and jumping
  • Progression system with unlockable content
  • Horse care and bonding mechanics
  • Customizable equipment and appearance options

How to Play

  • WASD or arrow keys to control your horse's movement
  • Spacebar to jump over obstacles
  • Shift key to adjust speed (walk, trot, canter, gallop)
  • E key to interact with objects or care for your horse
  • Q key to access special moves or techniques
  • Tab key to view objectives and map
  • Mouse to look around and adjust camera view
  • 1-4 keys to select different equipment or actions
