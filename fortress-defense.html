Play Fortress Defense Now

Defend your fortress against waves of enemies in this strategic tower defense game. Build towers, upgrade defenses, and manage resources to protect your base - all directly in your browser!

About Fortress Defense

Fortress Defense is a challenging browser-based tower defense strategy game that tests your tactical thinking and resource management skills. Position defensive structures strategically, upgrade your towers, and prevent enemy waves from breaching your fortress walls.

With its engaging gameplay mechanics and progressive difficulty curve, Fortress Defense offers a deep strategic experience that will keep you planning and adapting your defenses. Each level presents new challenges and enemy types that require different defensive approaches!

Game Features

  • Classic tower defense gameplay with strategic depth
  • Multiple tower types with unique abilities and strengths
  • Progressive upgrade system for your defenses
  • Various enemy types with different resistances and behaviors
  • Resource management and economy system
  • Challenging levels with increasing difficulty

How to Play

  • Click on available plots to build defensive towers
  • Select towers to upgrade them when you have enough resources
  • Position towers strategically to maximize coverage
  • Adapt your defenses to counter specific enemy types
  • Manage your resources efficiently to afford upgrades
  • Survive all enemy waves to complete each level
