Narrow One Game Modes: How to Win CTF, TDM, FFA and Control Points

Each mode rewards different skills. Use these concise tactics to stack advantages and avoid common traps.

Team Deathmatch (TDM)

Goal: reach the kill limit first.

  • Stick together: isolate enemies and trade kills favorably.
  • Map pressure: hold mid lines and deny enemy exits from spawn.
  • Rotate after wipes: re-center before the enemy regroups.

Free For All (FFA)

Goal: personal survival and opportunistic eliminations.

  • Play the high ground and sound cues; avoid chasing deep into chaos.
  • Third-party fights: strike players who are exposed after exchanges.
  • Break contact early: reposition before you get sandwiched.

Capture the Flag (CTF)

Suggested roles: 1–2 stealth runners, 2–3 mid/defense.

  • Routes: main lane for speed, side lane for safety, wrap for surprise.
  • Defense: keep at least one player anchoring near flag; call enemy paths.
  • Escort: body-block arrows for the carrier and pre-clear choke points.

Control Points

Two-point theory: hold two points steadily instead of greedily taking three.

  • Rotations: move based on kill feed and minimap pressure.
  • Anchor roles: one player stabilizes each point; others flex to contest.
  • Deny angles: use elevation and cover to force unfavorable enemy paths.
