Play Squad Shooter Now

Join forces with teammates in Squad Shooter, a tactical team-based FPS that rewards coordination and strategy. Work together, communicate effectively, and outmaneuver your opponents in intense multiplayer battles!

About Squad Shooter

Squad Shooter is an immersive browser-based first-person shooter that emphasizes teamwork and tactical gameplay. Join a squad of players and work together to complete objectives, secure strategic positions, and defeat the opposing team in various well-designed battlefields.

With its focus on team dynamics and role specialization, Squad Shooter creates an engaging cooperative experience where communication and coordination are key to victory. Experience the thrill of well-executed team strategies and the satisfaction of working together toward a common goal - all directly in your browser!

Game Features

  • Team-based gameplay with role specialization
  • Multiple tactical game modes and objectives
  • Various weapons and equipment loadouts
  • Communication tools for effective teamwork
  • Detailed maps with strategic points and cover
  • Browser-based - no downloads required

How to Play

  • WASD or arrow keys to move
  • Left mouse button to shoot
  • Right mouse button to aim down sights
  • Spacebar to jump
  • R to reload your weapon
  • Tab to view scoreboard and team status
  • V to use voice communication (if available)
