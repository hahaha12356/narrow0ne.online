Test your reflexes in Geometry Dash Scratch, a rhythm-based puzzle game that challenges your timing and coordination. Navigate through obstacles synced to catchy music - directly in your browser!

About Geometry Dash Scratch

Geometry Dash Scratch is an exhilarating browser-based rhythm puzzle game that combines fast-paced action with geometric obstacles and catchy music. Guide your character through increasingly difficult levels where timing is everything and obstacles are synchronized to the beat.

With its simple yet challenging gameplay mechanics, Geometry Dash Scratch offers an addictive gaming experience that will test your reflexes and patience. Jump, fly, and flip your way through colorful geometric landscapes in this musical adventure!

Game Features

  • Rhythm-based gameplay synchronized to music
  • Multiple challenging levels with increasing difficulty
  • Colorful geometric visuals and effects
  • Simple one-button control mechanism
  • Practice mode to master difficult sections
  • Browser-based - no downloads required

How to Play

  • Click, tap, or press spacebar to jump
  • Time your jumps to avoid obstacles
  • Hold to fly when in flying mode
  • Follow the rhythm of the music for better timing
  • Practice difficult sections to improve your skills
  • Complete each level to unlock new challenges
