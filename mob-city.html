Play Mob City Now

Navigate the dangerous streets of Mob City, an urban crime shooter set in a gritty metropolis. Engage in intense shootouts, complete missions, and rise through the criminal ranks - all in your browser!

About Mob City

Mob City is an immersive browser-based first-person shooter that drops you into the heart of a crime-riddled urban environment. Navigate through detailed city streets, alleyways, and buildings as you take on rival gangs, complete missions, and establish your dominance in the criminal underworld.

With its gritty atmosphere and action-packed gameplay, Mob City offers an engaging urban crime experience with a variety of weapons, vehicles, and mission types. Experience the thrill of street-level combat and strategic criminal operations without any downloads required.

Game Features

  • Urban crime setting with detailed city environments
  • Variety of weapons and street fighting styles
  • Multiple mission types and objectives
  • Vehicle driving and combat mechanics
  • Territory control and gang warfare elements
  • Browser-based - no downloads required

How to Play

  • WASD or arrow keys to move
  • Left mouse button to shoot
  • Right mouse button to aim
  • Spacebar to jump or vault over obstacles
  • E to interact with objects and enter vehicles
  • Tab to view map and mission objectives
  • 1-9 keys to switch between weapons

Criminal Operations

💰 Heist Operations

Execute high-stakes robberies targeting banks, jewelry stores, and armored cars. Requires planning, precision, and perfect timing to maximize profits while minimizing casualties.

Operation Requirements:

  • • Gather intel on security systems and guard patterns
  • • Assemble specialized crew with necessary skills
  • • Plan escape routes and backup strategies

⚔️ Gang Warfare

Engage in territorial battles against rival gangs for control of valuable city areas. These large-scale conflicts require tactical coordination and overwhelming force.

Operation Requirements:

  • • Control key strategic points in the territory
  • • Eliminate enemy leadership and strongholds
  • • Defend captured areas from counter-attacks

🚚 Smuggling Runs

Transport illegal goods across the city while avoiding law enforcement and rival interference. Requires skilled driving and knowledge of police patrol patterns.

Operation Requirements:

  • • Choose appropriate vehicles for cargo size
  • • Plan routes avoiding police checkpoints
  • • Maintain discretion during transport

🎯 Target Elimination

Remove high-value targets including rival gang leaders, informants, and obstacles to your criminal empire's growth. Precision and discretion are paramount.

Operation Requirements:

  • • Study target routines and security details
  • • Choose optimal time and location for strike
  • • Escape without leaving evidence
