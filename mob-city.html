Play Mob City Now

Navigate the dangerous streets of Mob City, an urban crime shooter set in a gritty metropolis. Engage in intense shootouts, complete missions, and rise through the criminal ranks - all in your browser!

About Mob City

Mob City is an immersive browser-based first-person shooter that drops you into the heart of a crime-riddled urban environment. Navigate through detailed city streets, alleyways, and buildings as you take on rival gangs, complete missions, and establish your dominance in the criminal underworld.

With its gritty atmosphere and action-packed gameplay, Mob City offers an engaging urban crime experience with a variety of weapons, vehicles, and mission types. Experience the thrill of street-level combat and strategic criminal operations without any downloads required.

Game Features

  • Urban crime setting with detailed city environments
  • Variety of weapons and street fighting styles
  • Multiple mission types and objectives
  • Vehicle driving and combat mechanics
  • Territory control and gang warfare elements
  • Browser-based - no downloads required

How to Play

  • WASD or arrow keys to move
  • Left mouse button to shoot
  • Right mouse button to aim
  • Spacebar to jump or vault over obstacles
  • E to interact with objects and enter vehicles
  • Tab to view map and mission objectives
  • 1-9 keys to switch between weapons
