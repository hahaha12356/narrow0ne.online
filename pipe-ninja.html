Play Pipe Ninja Now

Challenge your puzzle-solving skills with Pipe Ninja, a captivating game where you connect pipe segments to create a continuous flow path. Each level presents a unique configuration of pipes that you need to rotate and arrange to complete the flow network!

About Pipe Ninja

Pipe Ninja is an addictive puzzle game that challenges you to create continuous flow paths by connecting pipe segments. In each level, you're presented with a grid containing pipe pieces that can be rotated and rearranged. Your goal is to create an uninterrupted path from the start point to the end point, allowing the fluid to flow through.

As you progress through the game, you'll encounter increasingly complex puzzles with limited moves, special pipe types, obstacles, and multiple flow paths to manage simultaneously. With its simple concept but deep gameplay, Pipe Ninja provides a perfect mental workout that tests your spatial reasoning and planning skills. The game features clean, vibrant visuals and intuitive controls, making it accessible for players of all ages while still offering a significant challenge.

Game Features

  • Over 100 increasingly challenging levels
  • Multiple pipe types with different connection points
  • Special pipes and mechanics (one-way valves, switches, etc.)
  • Limited moves in higher difficulty levels
  • Multiple flow paths to manage simultaneously
  • Time attack mode for an extra challenge
  • Clean, vibrant graphics with fluid animations
  • Suitable for all ages while offering genuine puzzle challenges

How to Play

  • Click or tap on pipe segments to rotate them
  • In some levels, drag pipes to different positions on the grid
  • Create an uninterrupted path from the start point (often blue) to the end point (often red)
  • Fill all required paths to complete the level
  • Watch out for special pipe types that behave differently
  • Plan your moves carefully in levels with limited moves
  • Use hints if you get stuck (available in most levels)
  • Try to beat your best time in completed levels
