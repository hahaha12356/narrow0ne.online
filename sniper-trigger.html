Play Sniper Trigger Now

Test your precision and patience in Sniper Trigger, a tactical browser-based shooting game that puts your marksmanship skills to the test. Take aim, hold your breath, and pull the trigger!

About Sniper Trigger

Sniper Trigger is an immersive browser-based first-person shooter focused on precision and tactical gameplay. Take on the role of an elite sniper as you navigate through challenging missions, eliminate targets, and accomplish objectives in various detailed environments.

With its realistic shooting mechanics, including bullet drop and wind factors, Sniper Trigger offers a depth of gameplay rarely seen in browser games. Plan your approach, set up the perfect shot, and experience the satisfaction of a well-executed mission without any downloads required.

Game Features

  • Realistic sniper gameplay with precision mechanics
  • Multiple challenging missions and scenarios
  • Various sniper rifles with different characteristics
  • Environmental factors affecting shots (wind, distance)
  • Tactical planning and stealth elements
  • Browser-based - no downloads required

How to Play

  • WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Right-click to look through your scope
  • Left-click to fire your weapon
  • Shift to hold breath and stabilize your aim
  • R to reload your rifle
  • C to change shooting position (standing/crouching/prone)
