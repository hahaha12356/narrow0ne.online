Play Egg Helix Now

Experience the addictive challenge of Egg Helix, where you guide eggs down a spiraling tower. Test your reflexes and timing as you navigate obstacles, collect bonuses, and try to get your fragile eggs safely to the bottom!

About Egg Helix

Egg Helix is a captivating puzzle game centered around a simple but addictive premise: guide eggs down a spiraling helix tower without letting them break. As the eggs descend, you'll need to rotate the tower to navigate around obstacles, avoid hazards, and collect power-ups and bonuses along the way.

The game starts simple but progressively increases in difficulty, introducing new challenges, obstacle types, and special egg variants that behave differently. With its colorful visuals, satisfying physics, and "just one more try" gameplay, Egg Helix offers a fun and engaging experience that's easy to learn but challenging to master. Play directly in your browser with no downloads required.

Game Features

  • Simple one-touch controls with depth of gameplay
  • Over 50 increasingly challenging levels
  • Various obstacle types that require different strategies
  • Special egg types with unique properties

  • Colorful, vibrant 3D graphics
  • Satisfying physics-based gameplay
  • Play directly in your browser - no downloads needed

How to Play

  • Click or tap to drop eggs from the top of the helix
  • Press and hold the left mouse button to rotate the helix
  • Release to stop rotating
  • Guide eggs around obstacles by timing your rotations
  • Collect golden eggs and power-ups for bonuses
  • Avoid breaking eggs on spikes and other hazards
  • Complete level objectives to progress to more challenging stages
  • Aim for high scores by delivering eggs quickly and safely
