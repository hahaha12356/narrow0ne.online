Play Agent Smith Now

Step into the shoes of an elite secret agent in Agent Smith, an action-packed spy shooter with stealth mechanics and exciting missions. Infiltrate enemy bases, eliminate targets, and save the world - all directly in your browser!

About Agent Smith

Agent Smith is an immersive browser-based first-person shooter that puts you in the role of an elite spy on high-stakes missions. Navigate through various covert operations, use stealth or go guns blazing, and utilize an arsenal of specialized gadgets to accomplish your objectives.

With its blend of action and stealth gameplay, Agent Smith offers a thrilling espionage experience with diverse mission types and environments. Experience the excitement of spy action movies directly in your web browser, with no downloads required.

Game Features

  • Action-packed spy missions with stealth mechanics
  • Variety of weapons and special agent gadgets
  • Multiple mission environments and objectives
  • Choice between stealth and direct combat approaches
  • Progressive difficulty with increasingly complex missions
  • Browser-based - no downloads required

How to Play

  • WASD or arrow keys to move
  • Left mouse button to shoot
  • Right mouse button to aim down sights
  • C to crouch for stealth movement
  • E to interact with objects and use gadgets
  • Q to switch between weapons and gadgets
  • F to perform takedown moves on unaware enemies
